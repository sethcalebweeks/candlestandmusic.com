title: 082 Still A Work In Progress

I've seen a lot of figures in my day \
I've tallied all the numbers one by one \
But I've never seen a figure like your face \
And your number is my favorite one 

If you want to climb a mountain \
In the rain or in the sun \
Know that I am with you \
Whatever storm may come \
Let's build a home together as we renovate the rooms \
It's still a work in progress but I'm here to see it through \
I'm still a work in progress but my heart belongs to you 

Remember when we drove to see the lights? \
To gaze upon Auroras in the night \
Dark was the horizon of the skies \
Dawning this "new era" in  our lives 

If you want to climb a mountain \
In the hail or in the sun \
Know that I am with you \
Whatever storm may come \
Let's build a home together as we renovate the rooms \
It's still a work in progress but there's room enough for two \
I'm still a work in progress, but I'm so in love with you

If you want to climb a mountain \
In the hail or in the sun \
Know that I am with you \
Whatever storm may come \

It's still a work in progress but there's glory shining through \
We are still a work in progress but He's making all things new
